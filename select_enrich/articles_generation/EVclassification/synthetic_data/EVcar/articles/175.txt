    <p>Corporate <strong>fleet management</strong> strategies are also adapting. These shifts are driven not only by environmental concerns but also by cost efficiency and the appeal of being seen as sustainable leaders.</p>

    <h3>Electric Vehicle Policy: Support Worldwide</h3>
    <p>The international support for <strong>electric vehicle policy</strong> is evidenced by tax incentives, subsidies, and research funding aimed at making EVs more affordable and technologically advanced.
