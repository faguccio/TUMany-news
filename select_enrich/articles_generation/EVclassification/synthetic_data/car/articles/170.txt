            <h2 class="article-header">Revolutionizing Roll Cages</h2>
            <p>Roll cages, once a staple of race cars, are now being incorporated into road vehicles to improve passenger safety during rollovers. The modern roll cage designs utilize lightweight materials and integrate seamlessly into the vehicle's structure, providing an added layer of protection without compromising on aesthetics.</p>
        </section>

        <section class="article-section">
            <h2 class="article-header">Advancements in Braking Systems</h2>
            <p>The evolution of braking systems has reached new heights with the integration of technologies such as Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) and Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD). These systems ensure optimal braking performance, reduce the risk of skidding, and enhance driver control during emergency stops.</p>
        </section>

        <section class="article-section">
            <h2 class="article-header">The Resurgence of Leaf Springs</h2>
            <p>While often overshadowed by coil springs, leaf springs are making a comeback due to their durability and load-bearing capabilities. Modern engineering techniques have improved their performance, making them an ideal choice for heavy-duty vehicles and off-road applications.</p>
        </section>

        <section class="article-section">
            <h2 class="article-header">Conclusion</h2>

The development of advanced roll cages, sophisticated braking systems, and resilient leaf springs highlights the commitment to making roads safer for everyone. These advancements are paving the way for a new era of automotive safety.</p>
