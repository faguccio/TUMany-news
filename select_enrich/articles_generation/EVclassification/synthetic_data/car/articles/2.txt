            <h2>Advanced Braking Systems</h2>
            <p>One of the most significant developments in vehicle safety is the <strong>Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)</strong>. This technology prevents the wheels from locking up during braking, maintaining tractive contact with the road surface. This system enhances vehicle control and reduces stopping distances on slippery surfaces.</p>
        </section>

            <h2>Enhanced Comfort Features</h2>
            <p>Modern vehicles also prioritize driver and passenger comfort. Ergonomically designed <strong>headrests</strong> provide neck support and reduce fatigue on long drives. Additionally, climate-controlled seats ensure a pleasant journey regardless of external weather conditions.</p>
        </section>
        <section>
            <h2>Steering and Handling Improvements</h2>
            <p>The introduction of the <strong>rack and pinion</strong> steering system has greatly improved the handling capabilities of vehicles. This system offers precise control and responsiveness, allowing for better maneuverability on winding roads and during parking.</p>
        </section>
        <section>
            <h2>Superior Sound Systems</h2>
            <p>Audio enthusiasts will appreciate the integration of high-quality <strong>subwoofers</strong> in modern cars. These speakers deliver deep, rich bass, transforming the vehicle into a mobile concert hall. Whether it's a road trip or a daily commute, the sound experience is unmatched.</p>
        </section>
