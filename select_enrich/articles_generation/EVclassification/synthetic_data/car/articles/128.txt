    <h3>Drum Brakes: The Comeback Kid</h3>
    <p>Once thought to be overshadowed by disc brakes, drum brakes are making a surprising comeback. Their robustness and cost-effectiveness have caught the attention of automotive manufacturers. Modern drum brakes are now being equipped with advanced materials and designs, providing enhanced stopping power for front-wheel drive vehicles.</p>

    <h3>ATVs: Versatility and Power</h3>

    <h3>The Role of Front-Wheel Drive</h3>
    <p>Front-wheel drive systems continue to be popular for their efficiency and simplicity. This makes them an ideal choice for urban driving, where maneuverability and fuel economy are key considerations.</p>
