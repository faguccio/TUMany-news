        <p>The inclusion of smart sun visors in UTVs is another leap forward. These visors adjust automatically to changing light conditions, protecting drivers and passengers from glare and UV rays while providing a clear view of the landscape.</p>

As demand grows, manufacturers are investing in sustainable materials and energy-efficient engines to meet consumer expectations without compromising performance.</p>
