        <p>One of the highlights of the show was the introduction of advanced cold air intake systems. Truck owners can expect significant improvements in performance, making these vehicles more capable than ever before.</p>

        <p>While heavy trucks dominated the show, sedans also made a mark with their focus on fuel efficiency. Several manufacturers unveiled new models boasting impressive mileage figures, challenging the perception that larger vehicles are inherently less efficient. These sedans feature state-of-the-art engine designs and aerodynamic shapes to maximize every drop of fuel.</p>

        <h2>Innovative Drive Shafts Revolutionize the Industry</h2>
        <p>Another noteworthy advancement came in the form of innovative drive shafts. Designed to handle increased torque and provide smoother rides, these new drive shafts promise to enhance durability and performance in both trucks and sedans. Experts predict that this technology will set a new standard for reliability in the automotive sector.</p>

        <p>Despite the growing trend towards electrification, the auto show made it clear that traditional vehicles are not going anywhere. With improvements in efficiency and performance, manufacturers are ensuring that trucks and sedans remain competitive and relevant in today's market.</p>
